About Me

My name is Taylor Unger. I am a digital nomad from Texas currently splitting my time between Vietnam and Turkey (not the closest of places). This time difference can make for some interesting meeting times, but I can roll with the punches.

I am an ICT integrator, helping international and local schools integrate technology into their curriculum and physical classrooms. Usually that means working with underfunded or remotely located schools. Last semester I worked with a school only an 50km (through jungle roads) from Hanoi, yet they still didn't have reliable internet in the village. I will gladly brag to my future grandkids how "back in my day" I traveled across a jungle to attend digital classes. When I am not working in education, I travel and work in technology.

I want to take this class to better know learn and facillitate others learning of web authorship. I can help students with WYSIWYG websites, but those that ask for more advanced web changes, I am not yet equipped with the proper tools. I hope to learn them here.
