Technology

I am an ICT integrator, helping international and local schools integrate technology into their curriculum and physical classrooms. Usually that means working with underfunded or remotely located schools. Last semester I worked with a school only an 50km (through jungle roads) from Hanoi, yet they still didn't have reliable internet in the village. I will gladly brag to my future grandkids how "back in my day" I traveled across a jungle to attend digital classes.
